Research Story
During the mini-challenge 2, I was required to make an application with a big idea: improve productivity. We started research on questioning what productivity is and who should be productive in this pandemic situation. After a long process, my team members and I realized one big problem that is happening in the young people generation, that problem is: many young people are not able to do basic daily skills such as washing, cooking, fixing cars, and fixing electronic goods. We conducted interviews with 10 college students, and they felt that there was a lack of access for them to learn these basic skills. The skills on YouTube seem very dull and consuming too much of their time. Therefore, we make the content contained in our application in the form of cards that can be read in a short time so that it is easy for them to learn these skills. Also, we got data that these college students often feel confused if they want to find certain skills because they don't know several skills or goods names. Therefore, we created a machine learning-based camera search system so that they can find out tips and tricks in our application based on camera search results.
