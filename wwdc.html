Research Story
I challenge myself in this project. When I heard that Apple held a swift student challenge on every WWDC, I was immediately excited to work on this challenge, even though I realized that at that time I had only learned the swift plus ten days of working time. I share my schedule very effectively:

  • 2 days for ideation
  • 2 days for design using sketch
  • 3 days for coding
  • 3 days for bug cleaning and submission preparation

    • Technical Story
    Fun Circuit Simulator is a simulator that can help lecturers to teach about electronic components with a fun method and gamification while doing School From Home during COVID-19 situation. Even though I failed and was not selected as a candidate, this project was a catalyst for me while learning swift. I used AVFoundation and simple swipe gestures in the process of working on this game. Here is the video of fun circuit simulator
